# Business Problem

## Portuguese
Este projeto simula um ambiente de Sales Operations para analisar o pipeline comercial, identificar gargalos, melhorar a visibilidade operacional e apoiar decisões de crescimento com base em dados.

## English
This project simulates a Sales Operations environment to analyze pipeline performance, identify bottlenecks, improve operational visibility, and support growth decisions through data.

## Why this matters
Sales teams, account managers, marketing, and customer success depend on reliable CRM data and consistent reporting to make better decisions.

## First Insight
Most leads are still in early stages, which may indicate low conversion efficiency or a need for better qualification.
